# Short-Form Repurposing Guide — Real Estate Reels & TikTok

## The Core Principle

A YouTube video is a knowledge mine. Every video has at least 3-5 moments that can stand
alone as a 30-60 second Reel or TikTok. The job of this guide is to extract those moments
and rebuild them as short-form scripts — not summaries, not teasers, but complete standalone pieces.

**Critical rule:** Each short-form script must work for someone who has NEVER seen the YouTube video.
Never say "as I mentioned in my longer video" or "watch the full video to learn more" as the hook.
The value must be self-contained.

---

## How to Identify the 3 Best Repurposing Moments

From the video topic or transcript, look for:

1. **The Surprising Stat or Fact** — Any number, data point, or market insight that would stop a
   buyer or seller mid-scroll. ("Only 12% of homes in [City] are under $500K right now.")

2. **The Counterintuitive Insight** — Something that challenges what most people assume about the
   market or the buying/selling process. ("Most buyers think they need 20% down. In [City] right now, that's not true.")

3. **The Step-by-Step Moment** — Any section of the video that walks through a clear process,
   checklist, or sequence that viewers can act on immediately. ("Here's the exact 3 steps we use to price a home in [City].")

4. **The Local Market Angle** — Anything specific to the agent's city that makes it hyper-relevant
   to someone searching for local real estate content.

5. **The Objection or Fear** — A common question, worry, or misconception the video addresses directly.

Pick the 3 strongest. Make sure they have genuinely different angles — not three versions of the same insight.

## Short-Form Script Structure (Every Script)

### Hook (First 3 Seconds — CRITICAL)
- First line must stop the scroll immediately
- Speak directly to a buyer, seller, or local homeowner
- Use the city name when possible
- Must answer: "Is this for me?" in under 3 seconds
- See hook formulas in `hook-formulas.md`

### Value Body (20-45 Seconds)
- Deliver ONE clear insight, tip, or piece of information
- Short sentences. Spoken rhythm. No run-ons.
- Mark [ON SCREEN TEXT: ___] for any stats, lists, or key phrases to display
- For list-style scripts: say the number first, then deliver each point punchy
  - "Three things [City] buyers always ask me. Number one..."
- For insight scripts: state the insight, then explain it simply
  - "Here's what most [City] sellers don't realize..."

### CTA (Final 5-10 Seconds)
- One action. Not three. Not "follow, like, comment, and share."
- Options: "Book a free call — link in bio" / "Follow for more [City] market updates" /
  "Drop a comment below" / "Link in bio for the free [resource]"
- Keep it low-pressure — they're not ready to buy yet

## Script Length Guide

| Length | Word Count | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| 15 seconds | ~40 words | Single stat, quick tip, strong opinion |
| 30 seconds | ~75 words | 2-3 point list, quick how-to, myth bust |
| 60 seconds | ~150 words | Step-by-step process, market update, story |

Default to 30-45 seconds unless the content specifically calls for longer. Shorter wins.

---

## On-Screen Text Guidelines

Every script should include [ON SCREEN TEXT: ___] suggestions in brackets. These display
while the agent is talking and reinforce the key points visually.

Use for:
- Stats and numbers: [ON SCREEN TEXT: Average DOM in [City]: 14 Days]
- List items: [ON SCREEN TEXT: Step 1: Get Pre-Approved]
- Emphasis phrases: [ON SCREEN TEXT: Most buyers don't know this...]
- CTAs: [ON SCREEN TEXT: DM "GUIDE" for the free checklist]

Keep on-screen text SHORT — 5-8 words maximum per line.

## Caption Structure (Each Reel/TikTok)

```
[Hook line — matches the video hook but written for reading, not watching]

[2-3 lines of context or value tease — make them want to watch]

[CTA line]

[Hashtags: mix of local + topic + audience — 8-12 total]
```

### Real Estate Caption Examples

WEAK: "New video about the [City] market! Check it out!"

STRONG: "Inventory in [City] just hit its lowest point since 2022.

Here's what that means if you're planning to buy or sell in the next 6 months — and exactly what I'd do in your position.

Link in bio to book a free call.

#[City]RealEstate #[City]Homes #HousingMarket2026 #FirstTimeHomeBuyer #[City]Realtor #RealEstateTips"

## The 3-Script Differentiation Test

Before delivering, verify the 3 scripts are genuinely different:

- [ ] Each script has a different hook TYPE (stat vs insight vs step-by-step vs opinion)
- [ ] Each script speaks to a potentially different viewer (buyer vs seller vs local homeowner vs investor)
- [ ] The opening lines of all 3 scripts are completely different
- [ ] You couldn't swap the hooks between scripts and have them still make sense

If any two scripts feel like the same content in different words — replace one.
