# Platform-Specific Rules — 2026 Algorithm & Content Guidelines

## Instagram (Reels & Captions)

### Algorithm Priorities (2026)
- Reels get 2x engagement of other post types
- Watch time and saves are the top ranking signals
- Shares to DMs are heavily weighted
- Hashtags still matter but less than 2024 — 3-5 targeted hashtags, not 30

### Content Rules
- **Captions:** 150-250 words, story-driven, end with a clear CTA (link in bio or "DM me")
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 relevant hashtags at the end (not in the middle of text)
- **CTA options:** "Link in bio to book a free call" / "DM me [topic] and I'll send it over" / "Follow for more [City] market updates"
- **Posting frequency:** 3-5 feed posts per week + daily Stories

### Caption Structure
```
[Hook — first sentence must stop the scroll]
[2-3 paragraphs of value/story — conversational, local tone]
[CTA — link in bio or simple DM ask]
[3-5 hashtags]
```

## LinkedIn

### Algorithm Priorities (2026 — "Anti-AI")
- **Conversation depth** > vanity metrics (comments that spark real discussion)
- **Dwell time** — how long someone stays on your post (longer posts can win here)
- **Niche relevance** — content about your specific expertise, not generic advice
- **External links suppressed ~60%** — NEVER put links in the post body
- Carousels get ~4.2% engagement rate (highest format)
- Native video teasers (30-60s) outperform link posts for YouTube promotion

### Content Rules
- **Posts:** 250-500 words, thought-leadership storytelling
- **No links in post body** — always "Link in my first comment"
- **Always include a repost ask** — framed for a local/community audience, not other agents. Use: "Repost this if you know someone thinking about buying or selling in [City]" or "Share this with anyone navigating the [City] market right now."
- **LinkedIn First Comment** is a separate deliverable — contains the URL + brief description
- **Posting frequency:** 3-5x per week
- **Hashtags:** Optional, 3-5 at very end if used

### Post Structure
```
[Hook — 2-3 short lines that appear above the fold]

[Extended story or thought leadership — multiple short paragraphs]

[Value delivery — frameworks, lessons, specific advice]

[CTA — Repost ask + "Link in my first comment"]
```

### First Comment Structure
```
[Action verb] here: [URL] — [1-2 sentence description of what they get]
```

## Facebook

### Algorithm Priorities (2026)
- 3 pillars: Immersive Video, Authentic Storytelling, Interactive Engagement
- Personal Profile + Business Page hybrid approach works best
- Groups are powerful for nurturing (attract → nurture via Group → convert)
- UGC and community content outperforms polished branded content
- Video content preferred but not required for every post

### Content Rules
- **Posts:** 150-300 words, community-oriented, conversational
- **Link placement:** In comments preferred (same algorithm logic as LinkedIn)
- **No repost ask** — not native Facebook behavior
- **No hashtags** — not a Facebook norm for this audience
- **Posting frequency:** 3-4 posts per week + daily Group activity

### Post Structure
```
[Hook — relatable opening that sounds like talking to a friend]

[2-3 paragraphs of story or value — warmer tone than LinkedIn]

[CTA — "Link to [resource] is in the comments."]
```

## YouTube (Description & Metadata Only — Not Video Script)

### Algorithm Priorities (2026)
- 5 primary ranking signals: Click-Through Rate, Watch Time, Engagement, Satisfaction, Relevance
- Shorts use "Explore and Exploit" model — tests with small audiences first
- Satisfaction > pure watch time (quality of engagement matters)
- Audio quality matters more than video quality (49% watch just for presenter)
- First 48 hours of performance determine long-term ranking

### SEO Description Rules
- First 150 characters appear in search results — front-load keywords
- Lead magnet link appears twice (top and bottom)
- Natural keyword integration throughout — not keyword stuffing
- Include specific numbers and stats
- 15 tags using the 5-tier hierarchy
- 6-15 hashtags at the end

## Cross-Platform Caption Adaptation

The same core message should feel native to each platform:

| Element | Instagram | LinkedIn | Facebook |
|---|---|---|---|
| Length | 150-250 words | 250-500 words | 150-300 words |
| Tone | Conversational, direct | Professional storytelling | Community, friendly |
| CTA | Link in bio or DM ask | Link in first comment + repost ask | Link in comments |
| Hashtags | 3-5 at end | 0-5 at end (optional) | None |
| Links | Never in caption | Never in post body | In comments |
| Engagement ask | Save, share, follow | Repost ask (agent audience) | Organic engagement |
| Story depth | Medium | Deep | Medium |
