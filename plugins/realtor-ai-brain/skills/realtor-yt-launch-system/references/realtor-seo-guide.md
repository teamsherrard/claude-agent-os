# YouTube SEO Guide — Real Estate Agent Videos

## What This Skill Produces

1. **Title Variations** (5 options)
2. **Full YouTube Description** (150-300 words)
3. **Short YouTube Description** (condensed)
4. **Keywords / Tags** (15 tags)
5. **Hashtags** (8)

---

## Title Formulas for Real Estate

The most important rule: **City first, topic second.** Local search intent is the primary traffic source
for real estate agent YouTube channels. People are searching "[City] housing market" or "buying a home
in [City]" — not generic real estate advice.

| Formula | Example |
|---|---|
| [City] [Topic]: [Promise] ([Year]) | "Calgary Housing Market 2026: What Buyers Need to Know" |
| Buying/Selling a Home in [City] [Year]: [Angle] | "Buying a Home in Austin 2026: What Nobody Tells You" |
| [City] Real Estate [Year]: [Insight] | "Calgary Real Estate 2026: Is It a Buyer's or Seller's Market?" |
| [Topic] in [City]: [Promise] | "First-Time Buyer Guide for [City]: Step-by-Step for 2026" |
| [Number] Things [Audience] Need to Know About [City Topic] | "5 Things Every First-Time Buyer Needs to Know About [City] Real Estate" |

### Title Rules
- City/market name in the title whenever possible
- Current year for search freshness
- Keep under 60 characters when possible
- Use "Realtors" OR "Real Estate Agents" interchangeably (both are searched)
- Deliver on the promise — no clickbait
- Speak to a specific audience: buyers, sellers, investors, or relocators

### Description Rules
- First 150 characters critical — they appear in search previews
- City name used naturally 3-5 times (not stuffed)
- Lead magnet link or CTA link appears twice (top and bottom)
- Bullet list of video topics for scannability
- End with a short local agent bio (this also helps with local search)
- Hashtags at the very end

## Keywords / Tags (15 Tags) — 5-Tier Hierarchy

### Tier 1 — Exact Match (2-3 tags): The precise local topic
- "[city] real estate 2026"
- "buying a home in [city]"
- "[city] housing market"

### Tier 2 — Broad Match (3-4 tags): Wider topic without city
- "real estate market 2026"
- "first time home buyer tips"
- "housing market update"

### Tier 3 — Niche/Audience Specific (3-4 tags): Target viewer type
- "[city] first time buyers"
- "real estate investing [city]"
- "relocating to [city]"

### Tier 4 — Platform/Format (2-3 tags): How people search for this content
- "real estate youtube 2026"
- "realtor youtube channel"

### Tier 5 — Long-Tail (2-3 tags): Specific how-to queries
- "how to buy a home in [city] 2026"
- "is [city] a good place to buy a home"
- "[city] real estate agent tips"

### Tag Rules
- First tag = primary local keyword
- Always include the current year
- Mix city-specific and broad tags
- Use "[city]" as a placeholder — always replace with the actual city
- 15 tags total — never more

## Hashtags (8)

Mix of:
- Local: #[City]RealEstate, #[City]Homes, #[City]Realtor
- Topic: #HousingMarket2026, #FirstTimeHomeBuyer, #RealEstateMarket
- Audience: #RealtorLife, #HomeBuying, #HomeSelling

Format: `#NoSpaces` — all one word, capitalize each word for readability.
