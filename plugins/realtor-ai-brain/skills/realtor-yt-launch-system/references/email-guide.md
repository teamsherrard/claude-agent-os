# Email Guide — Real Estate Agent Video Launch Email

## Purpose

This email goes to the agent's list on the day their YouTube video publishes. It's a single-purpose
email: get people to click and watch. It is NOT a newsletter. It is NOT a market report. It is a
direct, personal message from a local expert saying "I made this for you — go watch it."

---

## Email Structure

### Subject Line
3 options with different approaches:
- **Curiosity gap:** "[City] market just shifted — here's what I'm seeing"
- **Outcome promise:** "How to buy a home in [City] without overpaying (new video)"
- **Direct/personal:** "[First name], I made this video specifically for [City] buyers"

Rules:
- Under 50 characters ideal — never over 70
- City name in subject line whenever natural
- Avoid "New Video" as the only subject — too generic

### Preview Text
Complements the subject line — adds a second hook or expands the promise.
- 40-80 characters
- Not a repeat of the subject — adds new info or context

### Email Body (80-150 words)

Structure:
```
[Opening line — direct, no "Hey, hope you're doing great!" filler]

[1-2 sentences on why this video matters to them specifically]

[What they'll get from watching — 2-3 specific things, not vague promises]

[Direct CTA — one link, one action]

[Sign-off: "[Agent first name]" on its own line]

[P.S. — optional, for lead magnet or bonus]
```

### Tone Rules
- Warm but efficient — treat their inbox like it's valuable
- Write like you're emailing one specific person, not a list
- No generic filler: no "I'm so excited to share this" or "This one is a game-changer"
- Assume they're busy and get to the point immediately
- City-specific observations make it feel local and relevant

## 3-Day Follow-Up Email

Sent 3 days after the launch email to anyone who didn't open or click. Shorter, softer, different angle.
Never a repeat of the launch email — this one earns its second chance by offering something new.

### Subject Line Approach
Use curiosity or a direct question — not a re-pitch:
- "Did you catch this?" (simple, low-pressure)
- "Quick question for [City] buyers/sellers"
- "One thing most [City] buyers get wrong"

### Body Structure (60-100 words)
```
[1-sentence re-entry — acknowledge the video exists without restating the whole pitch]

[1-2 sentences — a new angle, a specific insight from the video, or a question that creates curiosity]

[Single CTA line — same link, different framing]

[Agent name]
```

### Example
Subject: Did you catch this?

I put together a video this week on what the [City] market actually looks like right now — not the headlines, the real numbers.

One stat surprised even me. Worth a 12-minute watch if you're thinking about making a move this year.

[VIDEO LINK]

[Agent Name]
