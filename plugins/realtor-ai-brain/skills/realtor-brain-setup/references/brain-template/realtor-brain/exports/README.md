# Exports — your deliverables archive
Everything the system makes for you lands here **and syncs to your Google Drive**
(`Realtor AI Brain → exports`), so it's always there — any session, any device.

Things that land here:
- Offer guidebooks ("Why Work With Me"), lead magnets
- Market-update packages, listing kits, tour scripts
- The welcome doc and other rendered documents

These are polished *outputs*, not the source of truth — the canonical data always lives in
`identity/` and `memory/`, so any of these can be regenerated. But they're yours and they persist
in your Drive.
