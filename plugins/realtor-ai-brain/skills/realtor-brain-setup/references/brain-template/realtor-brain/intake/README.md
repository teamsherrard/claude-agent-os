# Drop zone — import into your Brain

Drop any existing materials here (or just upload them in chat), then say **"import my materials"**:
- testimonials / reviews (docs or screenshots)
- your bio / "about me"
- past posts, emails, captions, video scripts (these teach the Brain your real voice)
- market reports / CMAs / area guides
- lead magnets, buyer/seller guides, workbooks, listing presentations

Claude reads what's here, shows you a summary, and only saves after you confirm — nothing is added blindly.
This is just a drop zone; your Brain's actual files live in `identity/` and `memory/`.
