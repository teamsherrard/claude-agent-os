# Search Guide — Finding Trending Real Estate Articles

## Source Priority

### Canada — National
- CREA (crea.ca) — monthly housing stats, press releases
- CMHC (cmhc-schl.gc.ca) — affordability, rental, housing supply reports
- Bank of Canada (bankofcanada.ca) — rate decisions and monetary policy
- BNN Bloomberg (bnnbloomberg.ca) — breaking real estate and economic news
- Globe and Mail (theglobeandmail.com) — real estate section
- CBC Real Estate (cbc.ca/news/business/real-estate)
- Financial Post (financialpost.com) — housing, mortgage, and economic news
- Better Dwelling (betterdwelling.com) — Canadian housing data analysis

### Canada — Local Boards (monthly stats releases)
- CREB (creb.com) — Calgary
- TRREB (trreb.ca) — Toronto
- REBGV (rebgv.org) — Vancouver
- REALTORS Association of Edmonton (realtorsofedmonton.com)
- OREB (oreb.ca) — Ottawa
- APCIQ (apciq.ca) — Montreal

### United States — National
- NAR (nar.realtor) — monthly housing stats, press releases
- Realtor.com Research (realtor.com/research)
- Zillow Research (zillow.com/research)
- HousingWire (housingwire.com)
- Inman News (inman.com) — industry news
- CoreLogic (corelogic.com) — data and analytics
- Freddie Mac (freddiemac.com) — mortgage rate surveys
- Federal Reserve (federalreserve.gov) — rate decisions

### United States — Regional
- State Realtor association press releases
- City-specific newspapers (Dallas Morning News, Austin American-Statesman, Miami Herald, etc.)
- Local MLS data releases

## What Makes a Good Article for This Skill

An article is usable if it passes ALL of these:

| Check | Pass | Fail |
|-------|------|------|
| Recency | Published within 7 days | Older than 7 days |
| Source | Named credible publication or board | Anonymous, blog, brokerage promo |
| Data | Contains at least one specific stat or number | All vague generalities |
| Local relevance | Specific to agent's city OR directly affects their market | Only relevant to another market |
| Reaction potential | Agent can add a take or interpretation | Just a neutral data dump |
| Link status | Verified working via web_fetch | 404, paywall, redirect |

---

## Freshness Rules

| Age | Status |
|-----|--------|
| 0-3 days | Ideal — always prioritize |
| 4-7 days | Acceptable |
| 8-14 days | Only for rate/policy news with no newer update |
| 15+ days | Never use |

---

## The Specificity Test

Before selecting an article, ask:
1. What is the single most compelling number or data point in this article?
2. Can the agent in [city] add meaningful context or a take to this?
3. Would a buyer or seller in [city] care about this this week?
4. Is there at least one specific stat, named source, or concrete finding?

If you cannot answer yes to all four — find a different article.

---

## Topic Balance Across a 5-Article Batch

Aim for this mix across every batch:
- 1-2 local market stats (city-specific data)
- 1 rate or mortgage news item
- 1 buyer or seller trend
- 1 national news with local angle
- 1 wildcard (development, policy, neighbourhood, affordability)

Never deliver a batch of 5 articles all on the same topic (e.g., 5 rate articles or 5 price articles).
Variety keeps the agent's content feed from feeling one-note.
