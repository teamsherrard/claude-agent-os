# USP Knowledge File Template — Claude Project Reference Document

## About This Document

This is the agent's offer and USP knowledge file. It is separate from their brand
persona file. Together, the two files give Claude the complete picture of who this
agent is, who they serve, and what they offer.

Written in third person. Structured for Claude to read, not for the agent or prospects.
Uploaded to the agent's Claude Project alongside their brand persona file.

---

## DOCUMENT STRUCTURE

---

# [AGENT FIRST NAME] — Offer & USP Knowledge File
*Last updated: [Month Year]*
*Read this alongside [Agent Name] — Brand Persona Knowledge File*
*This file covers what the agent offers and why clients hire them.*
*Do not use this file without also reading the brand persona file.*

---

## 1. CORE OFFER SUMMARY

A 2-3 sentence plain-English summary of what this agent offers.
This is the answer to "what does working with [agent name] get you?"

Example: "[Agent Name] is a full-service real estate agent in [city] who provides
[specific offer summary]. They are known for [key differentiator] and primarily serve
[avatar type] in the [price range / community] market."

---

## 2. BUYER OFFER (if applicable)

List every specific thing the agent offers to buyers. Each item:
- Short label
- One sentence of specifics

Format:
```
[Offer item]: [Specific description of what it includes and what it means for the buyer]
[Offer item]: [Description]
```

Examples:
- Free moving truck access: Agent has a branded moving truck available to all
  buyer clients at no charge for their moving day.
- Preferred mortgage broker network: Agent has 3 vetted mortgage brokers they
  refer exclusively — all offer preferred rates for the agent's clients.
- Legal fee coverage: Agent covers up to $[X] in legal fees for buyer clients
  on closing through their partnership with [law firm].
- New construction expertise: Agent has [X] years of experience with new builds
  in [city] and knows which builders have the strongest track records.

---

## 3. SELLER OFFER (if applicable)

Same format as buyer offer. Every specific thing included for sellers.

Examples:
- Professional photography and video: Included at no charge on every listing.
  Agent uses [photographer name/service] exclusively.
- Home staging consultation: Agent has a staging background/certification and
  personally advises on every listing before photos.
- Pre-listing inspection: Agent arranges and covers the cost of a pre-listing
  inspection for all seller clients.
- Targeted digital advertising: Agent runs paid social and Google ads for every
  listing with a minimum budget of $[X].

---

## 4. UNIVERSAL OFFER (applies to all clients)

Items that apply regardless of buyer or seller status.

Examples:
- Response time commitment: Responds to all calls, texts, and emails within [X hours]
- Vendor network: Full network of vetted lawyers, inspectors, stagers, contractors,
  and mortgage brokers available to all clients at preferred rates
- Dedicated support: [Describe support model — solo agent, team, assistant, etc.]
- Communication style: [How often they update clients, preferred method, etc.]

---

## 5. DIFFERENTIATORS

What makes this agent genuinely different from other agents in their market.
Written as specific statements, not marketing language.

Format:
```
[Differentiator label]: [Specific explanation of what it is and why it matters]
```

Examples:
- Neighbourhood specialization: [Agent name] has sold [X] homes in [specific
  community] over [X] years and knows every street, builder, and price trend
  in that area better than any other agent.
- Staging credential: Agent holds a [specific certification] in home staging and
  personally stages or advises on every listing — not outsourced.
- Relocation specialty: Agent has worked with [X] out-of-province/state buyers
  relocating to [city] and has a specific process for remote buyers including
  virtual tours, trusted local contacts, and area orientation calls.
- Investor network: Agent has direct relationships with [X] active investors in
  [city] and can often match a seller with a buyer before the property hits MLS.

## 6. RESULTS AND PROOF

Specific numbers and outcomes the agent has delivered. Use exactly what was shared.
Do not embellish or estimate. If no numbers were provided, note "agent did not provide
specific metrics — use the client story below."

Format:
```
[Metric]: [Number] — [one line of context]
```

Examples:
- Clients helped: [X] buyers and sellers over [X] years in [city]
- Average days on market: [X] days (market average: [X] days)
- Sale-to-list ratio: [X]% average across all listings
- Awards / recognition: [Specific award, year, awarding body]
