# Jordan — Target Avatars
*identity · always address content to a specific avatar, never a generic audience*

### Avatar 1 — The NW Calgary First-Time Buyer
- **Who they are:** Late 20s to mid 30s, often a couple, frequently relocating from BC or Ontario for affordability. Pre-approved, first purchase.
- **Why they're buying:** Tired of renting / priced out where they came from; want to own a starter detached or townhouse with room to grow.
- **What they're worried about:** Overpaying, buying in the "wrong" community sight-unseen, interest rates, hidden costs.
- **What they want from an agent:** Someone who educates them, isn't pushy, and knows the NW communities cold.
- **What they say after working with Jordan:** "You never made us feel rushed and you actually talked us out of two houses."
- **Common questions they ask:** "Which community is the best value?" "Is now a good time or should we wait?" "What will my monthly payment actually be?"
- **Why they hesitate:** Fear of buying at the top + uncertainty about which NW community fits their life.
- **Content that resonates:** Community comparisons, true cost of ownership, "is now the time" market reads.

### Avatar 2 — The Move-Up Family
- **Who they are:** Mid 30s to 40s, 1–2 kids, outgrowing their first home, selling and buying at once.
- **Why they're buying/selling:** Need more space / better schools; equity has built up in their starter home.
- **What they're worried about:** Timing the sale and purchase, carrying two properties, school catchments.
- **What they want from an agent:** A coordinated plan so they're not homeless or double-carrying.
- **What they say after working with Jordan:** "You made the sell-and-buy feel like one smooth move, not two stressful ones."
- **Common questions they ask:** "Do I buy or sell first?" "What's my home worth now?" "Which community has the schools we want?"
- **Why they hesitate:** Fear of selling then not finding the next home.
- **Content that resonates:** Sell-and-buy strategy, school/community spotlights, equity/market timing.

### Who Jordan does NOT want to work with:
Investors chasing the absolute cheapest flip, and clients who want an agent to just open doors and write whatever offer they say.
