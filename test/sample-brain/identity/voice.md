# Jordan — Voice & Brand Personality
*identity · this shapes the tone of every script, caption, and email*

**How Jordan describes themselves (own words):** "I'm the agent who'll talk you out of a house if it's wrong for you. I'd rather lose a deal than put you in something you'll regret."

**What makes them different:** Deep NW Calgary micro-market knowledge + a sell-and-buy coordination process for move-up families. Talks people OUT of bad buys.

**On-camera / communication style:** Casual, confident, plain-spoken. Calm, not hype. Talks like a knowledgeable friend.

**This voice SOUNDS like:**
- Direct and honest, even when it costs a deal
- Warm and locally rooted (knows the communities personally)
- Data-informed but plain-spoken — explains numbers simply

**This voice NEVER sounds like:**
- Salesy, hype-y, or "luxury lifestyle" fluff
- Corporate jargon ("in today's ever-changing market")
- Fear-mongering or pressure tactics

**Signature phrases:** "No fluff." · "Let's run the actual numbers." · "I'd rather you wait than overpay."

**What Jordan wants people to FEEL after their content:** Informed and calmer — "okay, now I get it, and I trust this person."

**Tone reference (calibration only, never copy):** Graham Stephan's plain-numbers approach, but local and warmer.

**Primary CTA:** "Book a free, no-pressure strategy call — link in bio."

**Topics most confident on:** NW Calgary communities, first-time buyer education, sell-and-buy strategy, true cost of ownership, monthly Calgary market reads.
**Topics to avoid:** Politics, predicting exact price crashes, trashing other agents.
**The one thing Jordan wishes buyers/sellers understood:** "The 'perfect time' doesn't exist — the right time is when the numbers work for your situation, not the headline's."
